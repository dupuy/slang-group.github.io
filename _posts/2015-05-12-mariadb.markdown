To support all Unicode characters in MariaDB 5.5+, you can set the encoding of
your VARCHAR columns to utf8mb4.

<a href="https://mariadb.com/kb/en/mariadb/unicode/">MariaDB's website explains Unicode</a>
in greater detail.
